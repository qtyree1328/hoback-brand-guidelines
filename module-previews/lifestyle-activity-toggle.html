Jackson Hole Activities

From pristine powder to sun-drenched trails, every season brings a new way to experience the extraordinary.

Winter Activities

Winter in Jackson Hole is more than a season -- it's a way of life. From the first snowfall to the last run of spring, the valley transforms into a world-class winter playground, framed by the iconic peaks of the Tetons.

Let our Club Concierge take care of every detail -- every activity below can be arranged for you.

Skiing

Ski Passes

As daily ski passes may be limited, we will assist you in lift passes for you and your family.

Ski Lessons

If you require full or half day lessons, please reach out to our Club Concierge.

Private Ski Fitting

Access private demo fleet and premium gear, all in the comfort and privacy of your residence.

Backcountry Ski Guiding

Beyond the resort gates into untouched terrain, with small-group adventures and lift access coordinated at booking.

Heli Skiing

The ultimate heli-skiing adventure in Jackson Hole, with small groups, epic terrain, and a full day of powder.

Cross-Country / Nordic Skiing

Explore over 14 miles of groomed Nordic trails in Grand Teton National Park with expert-led or self-guided tours.

Other Winter Activities

Snowmobiling

Guided or self-guided tours through Yellowstone, Grand Teton, or the Gros Ventre, with all gear and transport.

Sleigh Rides

Vintage horse-drawn sleigh through crisp mountain air, with sunset rides and optional fireside apres.

Wildlife Safaris

Full- or half-day tours timed for optimal sightings during early mornings or golden hour.

Dog Sledding

Experience the thrill of dog mushing through Wyoming's stunning wilderness from Teton Village.

Snowshoeing

Guided full- or half-day tours with custom routes, all gear provided, and optional wellness experiences.

Snowcoach

Explore Yellowstone's Old Faithful and geothermal wonders in a luxurious snowcoach adventure.

Ice Skating

Scenic outdoor skating at Teton Village's Warming Hut rink with breathtaking mountain views.

Tubing

Thrilling toboggan, tubing, and alpine slide rides at Jackson Hole's family-friendly terrain park.

Alpine Climbing

World-class alpine climbing in the Tetons with technical ascents and custom itineraries.

Summer Activities

As the snow melts and wildflowers bloom, Jackson Hole awakens to sun-drenched days and endless adventure. From fly-fishing on the Snake River to horseback rides, every moment is framed by the towering Tetons.

Your Club Concierge can arrange the best guides for you and your family. All activities below can be seamlessly coordinated.

Wildlife Safaris

Expert naturalist-guided safaris through Yellowstone or Grand Teton with transportation and gourmet lunches.

Horseback Riding

Private, guided rides through alpine meadows with expert wranglers and optional gourmet cast-iron cooking.

Whitewater Rafting / Scenic Floats

Jackson Hole's Snake River offers daily full- or half-day trips for all ages and skill levels.

Fly-Fishing

World-class guided fly fishing on the Snake, Green, Salt Rivers, or local spring creeks.

Hiking Jackson Hole Valley

From Teton Village to tranquil trails like Phelps Lake or Hidden Falls within the nearby national parks.

Other Summer Activities

Climbing / Via Ferrata

Explore the Tetons with expert guides or take on the thrilling Via Ferrata just steps from your door.

Mountain Biking / Ebike Tours

Breathtaking scenic views on uncrowded trails. Our Club Concierge will arrange rentals or scenic tours.

ATV / Side-By-Sides Tours

Adventure through the Gros Ventre wilderness and Curtis Canyon, uncovering hidden gems.

Golf & Tennis

Access three private golf courses exclusively to the Hoback Club.

Wooden Boat Floats

Private Snake River float with chef-prepared meals and sunset views.

Natural Hot Springs

Journey to secluded hot spring oasis by car, snowmobile or dog sled. Full or half day excursions.

Helicopter Tours

Private helicopter or Kodiak flight over Jackson Hole with custom routes and optional champagne toasts.

Hot Air Balloon Tours

Serene 3-hour balloon experience over the Tetons at first light with celebratory landing.

Paragliding

Tandem flight above Jackson Hole and the Teton Range -- no experience needed, ages 6 and up.

Stargazing

Year-round evening tours with astronomy experts, top equipment, cozy blankets, and gourmet beverages.
